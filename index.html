About Us

Building India's Future with Quality Steel

25+ years of excellence in steel manufacturing

Excellence in Steel Manufacturing Since 1998

Natraj Steel has been at the forefront of India's steel industry for over 25 years. We combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver steel products that meet the highest international standards.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities span over 50 acres and are equipped with the latest German and Japanese technology. We produce a comprehensive range of steel products including TMT bars, structural steel, and specialized industrial steel that powers India's infrastructure growth.

With a production capacity of 500,000 tons annually, we serve over 50 countries worldwide while maintaining our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification and multiple industry awards stand testament to our unwavering dedication to excellence.

Foundation & Vision

Natraj Steel was established with a vision to revolutionize India's steel industry. Started with a small facility producing 10,000 tons annually, laying the foundation for future growth.

ISO Certification
