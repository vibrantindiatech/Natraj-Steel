Our Legacy

25+ years of excellence in steel manufacturing and innovation

A Legacy of Strength and Reliability

Founded in 1998, Natraj Steel has grown from a modest manufacturing unit to one of India's leading steel producers. Our journey is marked by consistent innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and a deep understanding of our customers' needs.

Today, we operate state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities equipped with the latest German and Japanese technology, producing over 500,000 tons of premium steel annually for domestic and international markets.

Our ISO 9001:2015 certification and multiple industry awards stand testament to our dedication to excellence and our role in building India's infrastructure backbone.
